Sabzi Polo (persian herb rice)

source: Mummum's cookbook (pg 188)

Ingredients (6 servings):
- 3 cups basmati
- 1/2 cup chives or scallions
- 2 cups dill
- 2.5 cups parsley
- 2 cups cilantro
- 3 cloves garlic
- ghee, oil, yogurt (depending on tahdig technique)
- saffron
- whole green garlic
- 1 tsp cinnamon

Directions:
1) make rice same as tahdig recipe, however:
2) after draining rice and creating tahdig layer, layer rest of rice with herbs, garlic and spices

Serve:
1) serve with white fish (mahi) as is traditional for nowruz
