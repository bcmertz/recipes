Ginger Beer

Ingredients:

- Ginger
- Sugar
- Water

Spices:
- cinnamon
- cayenne
- clove
- star anise
- cardamom
____

 Bug:
  1- 2 cups water, 2 Tbsp sugar and ginger
  2- Feed 2 Tbsp daily sugar and ginger
  3- ready when yeasty, foamy, and active

 Beer:
  1- simmer 100g ginger, 100g sugar, 1L water 10 - 15 minutes with spices
  2- cool and add 1 cup bug
  3- put in sanitized flip top bottles with ample headspace
  4- burp daily for 1 week then refrigerate
