Faarikaal (Norwegian lamb and cabbage stew)

source: papa's mom

Ingredients:
- 1kg lamb (2 lb)
- 3 tsp salt
- 1.5 kg cabbage (3 lb)
- 12 peppercorns
- 4 Tbsp flour
- 1/2 L water (4 cups)
- [optional] parsley

Directions:
1) wash meat
2) save boniest and fatties pieces for bottom of the pot
3) layer meat, cabbage, meat, etc
4) salt and flour peppercorns between each layer
5) add water, cook 1.5 - 2 hours

Serve:
- with fresh parsley
- ready when the meat is very tender
