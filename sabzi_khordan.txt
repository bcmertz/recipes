Sabzi Khordan

Ingredients:

- tarragon
- radish
- green onion
- chives
- cilantro
- parsley
- dill
- mint
- basil
- feta
- pita
____

 Directions:

  1- Trim stems
  2- soak radish and green onion in cold water
  3- slice radish
  4- cut off green onion whites and split in half
  5- cut green part into small sections
  6- soak in water 10 min and drain
  7- arrange in platter with feta slices and pita
