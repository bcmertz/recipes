Chinese steamed fish

source: Zoe's boyfriend
* good alternative recipe https://thewoksoflife.com/steamed-whole-fish
  - veggies / sauce cooked separately, kinda seems nice

Ingredients:
- large white fish filleted
- ginger
- scallion
- cilantro

Directions:
1) steam fish
   - cassarole inside large closable container with water on bottom
   - on top of fish steam place julienne ginger and scallions and cilantro
   - beneath fish make soy sauce and toasted sesame oil
   - 9 minutes maybe

Serve:
- serve with more julienne ginger and scalliens and cilantro that have had hot oil poured over top
- perhaps serve with sabzi polo
- sprinkle some lime juice on top
