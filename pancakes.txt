Servings: 8

Ingredients:
- 1.5 cups flour
- 3.5 tsp baking powder
- 1 Tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1.25 cups milk
- 3 Tbsp melted butter
- 1 egg

Directions:
1) sift dry ingredients together
2) beat in wet ingredients
3) cook first side until bubbly then flip
4) nushe joon
