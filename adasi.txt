Adasi (Persian Lentils)

source: https://thepomegranatediaries.wordpress.com/2012/11/19/adasi-persian-style-lentils
[TODO] merge mommom recipe

Ingredients: (Serves 4)
3 tbsp canola oil
1 large onion, thinly sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp turmeric
1 cup dried green lentils, rinsed
3 cups vegetable broth (or chicken broth)
1 tsp golpar (ground angelica powder)*
1 tsp ground cinnamon
salt and pepper

Directions:
1) fry onions until soft and golden, add turmeric and garlic and fry another minute
2) add lentils and vegetable broth, bring to boil then simmer
3) add water as needed until lentils are very soft
4) add golpar, cinnamon, salt and pepper, nush-e jan

Notes:
- thicken than soup but not too dry
