Mahi (persian white fish)

source: Mummum's cookbook (pg 188)

Ingredients (6 servings):
- 1 large white fish (striped bass) 3lbs
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- olive oil
- juice from oranges or limes

Directions:
1) dust washed and dried fish fillets with flour/seasoning mix
2) brown on medium heat in 4 Tbsp oil

Serve:
1) sprinkle fish with citrus before serving
2) drizzle any remaining saffron water from rice on fish
3) serve with sabzi polo (herb rice) as is traditional for nowruz
