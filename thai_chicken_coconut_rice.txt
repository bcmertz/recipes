Thai chicken thighs with coconut rice

source - https://www.bonappetit.com/recipe/thai-roast-chicken-thighs-with-coconut-rice

Ingredients (2 servings / cup rice):
- 1 lime (zest and juice)
- Ginger (1" peeled, finely grated, plus 3 peeled slices)
- 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- ⅓ cup coconut palm sugar or (packed) light brown sugar
- ¼ cup fish sauce
- 1 13.5-oz. can unsweetened coconut milk, divided
- 2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for brushing
- 2 tsp. freshly ground black pepper, plus more
- 2 lb. skin-on, bone-in chicken thighs (4–6)
- ½ medium head of green cabbage, stem trimmed, sliced into 1"-thick wedges
- ½ tsp. Diamond Crystal or ¼ tsp. Morton kosher salt, plus more
- 1 cup white jasmine rice, rinsed until water runs clear
- Cilantro leaves with tender stems and lime wedges (for serving)

Rice:
1) Bring rice, ginger slices, 1 cup coconut milk, remaining 1/4 tsp Morton Kosher salt, and 1 cup water to boil
2) Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until liquid is absorbed (10-15 minutes)
3) Let rice sit until ready to serve

Directions:
1) Preheat oven 400°F
2) Mix lime zest and juice, grated ginger, garlic, coconut palm sugar, fish sauce, ½ cup coconut milk, 2 Tbsp. oil, 2 tsp. pepper
3) Reserve ¼ cup marinade for serving.
4) Marinade chicken in fridge 1-12 hours
5) Brush cabbage wedges with oil, salt and pepper, and arrange cut side down in cast iron skillet
6) Place chicken skin side up on top, season with salt and pepper
7) Roast 35 minutes
8) Increase oven to 450°F until thighs are brown and crispy (5-7 minutes)
9) Serve with coconut rice, marinade, lime wedges, and cilantro
