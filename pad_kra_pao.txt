Pad Kra Pao

Source (https://hot-thai-kitchen.com/holy-basil-stir-fry/#tasty-recipes-6663-jump-target)
Ingredients:

- 5-10 Thai chilies
- 5 cloves garlic
- Mild red pepper
- 1/2 cup long beans
- 1/2 small onion
- 300g tofu
- 1 Tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 Tbsp soy sauce
- 1 Tbsp fish sauce
- 1.5 cups holy basil
- 2-3 eggs
- Rice
- Lime
____

 Directions:

  1- Pound chilies into fine paste with garlic
  2- Combine soy oyster and fish sauce
  3- Saute garlic-chili paste over medium high heat till garlic is golden
  4- Mix in tofu, diced onions and diced long beans
  5- Remove from heat and stir in cut up basil
  6- Plate over rice with fried egg side
  7- Garnish with Prik Nam Pla: fish sauce, lime juice, chopped chilies, and fresh minced garlic
