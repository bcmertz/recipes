Pan Seared Fish

Ingredients:

- Fish
- Flour
- Breadcrumbs (optional)
- Salt, pepper

Marinade:
- ginger
- garlic
- onion
- toasted sesame oil
- vinegar
- olive oil
- salt
- soy sauce
- honey
- citrus

____

 Directions:

  1- Marinade fish
  2- Coat in flour
  3- Shallow fry/saute fish, adding oil as needed
